Grilled Cheese Recipe



Welcome to my favorite grilled cheese recipe! I'm going to share with you guys how to make it and I hope you enjoy it as much as I have!

The final product should look something like the image below.




Recipe


Ingredients:


  • 2 slices 1/2”-thick Pullman or other white bread
  • 2 tbspoons mayo
  • 1 tbspoon unsalted butter
  • 2 ounces thinly sliced American or cheddar cheese (about 4 slices)
  • Freshly grounded black pepper
  • Tomato soup (optional; for serving)

Instructions:


  1. Place bread on a cutting board and spread mayonnaise over top side of each; this is key to a golden, delectable crunch.
  2. Heat a small skillet (nonstick, ideally) over medium.
  3. Slide in half of butter.
  4. When it melts, place 1 slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, in skillet; top with cheese; season with pepper.
  5. Top with second slice of bread, mayonnaise side up.
  6. When underside is golden brown, about 4 minutes, turn sandwich and add remaining butter to skillet.
  7. Press down on sandwich to encourage even browning and to help melt cheese—be gentle, don't smash it.
  8. Cook until second side is golden brown and cheese is melted. Eat immediately, preferably with soup.
